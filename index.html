Particle simulation

Light 2d renderer written in c. Particles with simulated gravity on a large stationary mass in the center.

Capsule collider

Collision detection algorithm to solve the closest points between a sphere swept line and basic primitives.

Movement test with capsule collider

Quake style movement as a proof of concept with the above collision detection.

Procedural character animation

Also uses the above mentioned collision detection. Animations are fully procedural with inverse kinematic joints.
